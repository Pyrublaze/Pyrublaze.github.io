Political Orientation

Ideology and Personality

 

Right-wingers tend to have lower openness and higher conscientiousness compared to left-wingers. There is also some evidence suggesting right-wingers also tend to have lower neuroticism and agreeableness, and higher extraversion. The correlation between personality traits and political orientation is a function of an innate common genetic factor, as opposed to being causal in one direction or another. Conservatives and liberals also have equal levels of narcissism, although they differ in the ways in which narcissists of each orientation tend to express their narcissism.

 

Narcissism and Political Orientations

  • Finds that conservatives and liberals are equally narcissistic, although differ in their narcissistic traits.
  • Conservatives are more entitled, while liberals are more exhibitionistic.

Personality and political orientation: Meta-analysis and test of a Threat-Constraint Model

  • Meta-analysis
  • Conservatism was weakly negatively associated with openness and weakly positively associated with conscientiousness.
  • The association between openness and political orientation varied substantially among levels of systemic threat and instability as indexed by unemployment and national homicide rates, with a moderate association when systemic threat was low and a low association when systemic threat was high.
  • Neuroticism was negatively associated with political conservatism
  • There was no relationship between agreeableness or extraversion and political orientation

Correlation not Causation: The Relationship between Personality Traits and Political Ideologies

  • Uses a causal model to assess the relationship between personality traits and political ideologies
  • Finds that the correlation between the two is a function of an innate common underlying genetic factor, as opposed to personality causing someone to adopt specific political ideologies

Big Five Personality Traits and Political Orientation: An Inquiry into Political Beliefs

  • Extraversion is weakly positively associated with conservatism.
  • Agreeableness and neuroticism are positively associated with liberalism

Ideology and Intelligence

 

Libertarians tend to be the most intelligent, followed by liberals and then conservatives. This appears to be due to beliefs in personal and economic liberalism both being correlated with intelligence. However, this effect varies across cultures, and there is evidence to suggest that a lot of the relationship between ideology and intelligence is a function of which beliefs are mainstream, with mainstream beliefs being associated with higher intelligence. More intelligent people tend to be more ideologically intolerant.

 

Cognitive Ability and Political Preferences in Denmark

  • Personal and economic liberalism were weakly positively correlated with intelligence

The Association of Cognitive Ability with Right–Wing Ideological Attitudes and Prejudice: A Meta–Analytic Review

  • Meta-analysis
  • Finds that lower cognitive ability is associated with a stronger endorsement of right-wing attitudes and greater prejudice

Do Smarter People Have More Conservative Economic Attitudes? Assessing the Relationship Between Cognitive Ability and Economic Ideology

  • Finds that higher cognitive ability is associated with conservative views on economic issues

Partisan ideological attitudes: Liberals are tolerant; the intelligent are intolerant

  • Finds that more intelligent people are more ideologically intolerant than the less intelligent

Verbal intelligence is correlated with socially and economically liberal beliefs

  • Finds that individuals who identify as republican have slightly higher verbal intelligence than those who identify as democrat, by 2-5 IQ points
  • This can be reconciled with research suggesting that conservatives are less intelligent than liberals as higher intelligence among classically liberal republicans who are both socially and economically liberal compensates for lower intelligence among socially conservative republicans.

Political orientations, intelligence and education

  • Study in Brazil
  • Mean IQ of those center-right was 105, followed by centrists at 103, center-left at 101, clearly left at 98, and right at 95.
  • Intelligence is positively related to caring about politics

Intelligence and Politics Have a Complex Relationship

  • Research has suggested that cultural context plays a role in the relationship between intelligence and politics, as in South Africa where right-wing values are the social norm right-wingers have higher intelligence
  • In places like Brazil where extremism is in its history, people might be wary of extreme views and thus may support more center-leaning perspectives.
  • Categorizing people are simply generally liberal or conservative in studies on intelligence and politics may mask differences in people’s views on social or economic issues, where social issues are given more weight than liberal issues.

Understanding Libertarian Morality: The Psychological Dispositions of Self-Identified Libertarians

  • Looks at the psychology of libertarian morality
  • Finds that libertarians showed stronger endorsement of individual liberty as a guiding moral principle and lower in all others, a relatively cerebral rather than emotional cognitive style, and lower interdependence and social relatedness
  • Libertarians did better on a cognitive reflection task compared to liberals and conservatives, which measures intelligence and a person’s ability to suppress an intuitive response in service of the cognitive reasoning required to solve the problems

Intelligence makes people think like economists: Evidence from the General Social Survey

Intelligence Makes People Think Like Economists: Further Evidence

  • People who are more intelligent tend to have more libertarian economic views

Ideology and Well-Being

 

Conservatives tend to be happier, have more meaning in life, and are healthier. These effects are due to a number of causes, most notably a belief in personal agency being a cause of the happiness and health effects.

 

Conservatives are happier than liberals, but why? Political ideology, personality, and life satisfaction

  • Political conservatives are happier than liberals
  • This is due to belief in personal agency, more positive outlook on life, more transcendent moral beliefs (religiosity, moral clarity, intolerance of transgressions), and a generalized belief in fairness.

Conservatives Report Greater Meaning in Life Than Liberals

  • Conservatives report they have greater meaning in life

Political orientation and physical health: The role of personal responsibility

  • Political conservatism is associated with greater physical health through it promoting personal responsibility

Conservatives report, but liberals display, greater happiness

  • Political conservatives report greater happiness, but liberals more frequently used positive emotional language in their speech and smiled more intensely and genuinely in photographs

Are republicans healthier than democrats?

  • Republicans are less likely to smoke and report poor health and democrats and independents

Ideology and Physical Appearance

 

Right-wingers tend to be more attractive than left-wingers, partially due to attractive people being more likely to support inequality, as they earn more. Appearance is a substantial predictor of political ideology, as facial recognition technology can correctly identify political orientation in liberal-conservative face pairs significantly greater than at random.

 

Facial recognition technology can expose political orientation from naturalistic facial images

  • Facial recognition technology can correctly classify political orientation in 72% of liberal-conservative face pairs, better than human accuracy which is 55% or a personality questionnaire (66%). Accuracy was at 69% when controlling for age, gender, and ethnicity.

Mirror, mirror on the wall, who’s the fairest of them all? Thinking that one is attractive increases the tendency to support inequality

  • Self-perceived attractiveness increases one’s tendency to support inequality

Effects of physical attractiveness on political beliefs

  • After controlling for SES, more attractive individuals are more likely to report higher levels of political efficacy, identify as conservative, and identify as republican.

The right look: Conservative politicians look better and voters reward it

  • Conservative politicians tend to be more attractive, which increases their success in elections
  • The former finding is consistent with the idea that beautiful people earn more which makes them less inclined to support redistribution

Mental illness and the left

  • Left-wingers tend to be more mentally ill than right-wingers, even after controlling for age, sex, and race.

How to Understand the Well-Being Gap between Liberals and Conservatives.

  • Ideological differences in depression are significantly greater than gender differences.
  • This gap has consistently remained across decades
  • Conservatives have better sex lives than liberals
  • The ideology gap in mental illness remains significant even after controlling for religious attendance
  • Liberals are far more likely to be depressed, anxious, or otherwise neurotic compared to conservatives, and are roughly twice as likely as conservatives to report being diagnosed with a mental illness
  • Both political ideology and psychological dispositions and disorders are heritable, so it could be that this link derives from shared genetic links, or it could be due to the adoption of either ideology causing one to have better or worse mental health
  • There is evidence that children who are maladjusted (angry, aggressive, or otherwise antisocial) are more likely to align themselves with left-wing parties. Those who experienced abuse, insecurity, and trauma as children are also much more likely to identify as liberal.
  • People who score highly on dark triad characteristics may be especially likely to gravitate towards certain strands of social justice ideology and authoritarianism, although some right-aligned ideology and social movements also attract some people. It appears that due to this these people tend to simply gravitate to areas where they can enhance themselves or exert influence, which seems more common in cultural left views which are more institutionalized.
  • In more liberal countries, and more liberal regions within countries, there are higher rates of mental disorders
  • Those who are right-wing and religious tend to give most frequently, give the highest shares of their income, and donate specifically to causes to alleviate human poverty and suffering
  • However, liberals have more moral concern and tend to feel higher levels of empathy.
  • Liberals tend to be less emotionally stable, and are far more likely to prize emotionality and to dwell on their emotions
  • Liberals have more politically homogenous communities and social networks, and are far more willing to avoid, break off, or curb relationships over political differences, which is unlikely to enhance one’s happiness or well-being
  • One argument is that leftists are more likely to report being mentally ill because their spaces have a culture surrounding seeking out help even when problems are mild
  • Liberals are more anxious about interactions across difference, and their perceptions, judgements and behaviors change dramatically depending on the demographic characteristics of the people they are engaging with or referring to, while conservatives are generally consistent
  • Heightened perceptions of bias and discrimination are associated with mental anguish, social strain, and adverse physical outcomes, and liberal ideology is associated with heightened perceptions of bias and discrimination
  • Among racial and ethnic minorities, perceptions of discrimination correlate strongly with educational attainment, which itself strongly informs ideological lean, which means that socioeconomic status has diminishing mental health returns for minorities
  • Research suggests that the challenges of dealing with white liberal peers and their neuroses and dispositions may be a key source of burnout for minorities in progressive spaces
  • It’s been argued that many strains of liberal ideology function in practice as a form of reverse cognitive behavioral therapy
  • However, none of this tells us about the truth of liberalism or conservatism. Arguably, deep commitment to “the facts” may be an impediment to flourishing

Heritability of Political Views

 

Political views appear to be roughly 40% heritable, if not more. This varies heavily with political sophistication, with people high in political sophistication having an estimated heritability of political views of 74%. However, there is significant genetic overlap between political views and a variety of psychological traits.

 

Genetic Influences on Political Ideologies: Twin Analyses of 19 Measures of Political Ideologies from Five Democracies and Genome-Wide Findings from Three Populations

  • Finds an aggregate estimate that 40% of the variance in social and political attitudes were explained by genetic influences

Genes, Ideology, and Sophistication

  • Estimates a 57% population-level heritability for socio-political conservatism
  • This varies heavily with political sophistication. Heritability is 74% for top fifth of population in political sophistication and 29% for bottom fifth

Study on twins suggests our political beliefs may be hard-wired

On the genetic basis of political orientation

  • Political orientation has a notable genetic basis, although there is genetic overlap between political orientation and psychological traits.
  • This includes cognitive ability, social fear, big five personality, need for cognition and need for cognitive closure, need to evaluate, religiosity, social dominance orientation, and right-wing authoritarianism
