Homosexual Conversion Therapy

 

Conversion therapy for homosexuals doesn’t appear to work for the most part, although there are some exceptions. It is linked with depression, suicidality, anxiety, social isolation, and decreased capacity for intimacy on the whole. Amongst those who are dissatisfied with their homosexuality, the picture is more positive, but it is nonetheless negative in general.

 

It should be noted that the negative effects of conversion therapy are likely to be understated if it as a widespread practice as those who go to conversion therapy now are more likely to have strongly conservative religious views, so conversion therapy for the general homosexual population would have even worse effects than those found in most studies.

 

What does the scholarly research say about whether conversion therapy can alter sexual orientation without causing harm?

  • Literature review of 47 studies
  • Finds that the aggregate of studies overwhelmingly finds that conversion therapy is not only ineffective, but is linked with depression, suicidality, anxiety, social isolation and decreased capacity for intimacy.

Retrospective Self-Reports of Changes in Homosexual Orientation: A Consumer Survey of Conversion Therapy Clients

  • Survey of 882 dissatisfied homosexuals, most of whom underwent conversion therapy
  • Most considered themselves at least mostly homosexual.
  • Those who underwent conversion therapy reported having decreases in the frequency of homosexual thoughts and fantasies due to their therapy
  • They also reported large improvements in psychological, interpersonal, and spiritual well-being.

National Survey on LGBTQ Youth Mental Health 2019

Appropriate Therapeutic Responses to Sexual Orientation

  • “The American Psychological Association Task Force on Appropriate Therapeutic Responses to Sexual Orientation conducted a systematic review of the peer-reviewed journal literature on sexual orientation change efforts (SOCE) and concluded that efforts to change sexual orientation are unlikely to be successful and involve some risk of harm, contrary to the claims of SOCE practitioners and advocates”
  • “the task force concluded that the population that undergoes SOCE tends to have strongly conservative religious views that lead them to seek to change their sexual orientation.”

Parent-Initiated Sexual Orientation Change Efforts With LGBT Adolescents: Implications for Young Adult Mental Health and Adjustment

  • “Attempts by parents/caregivers and being sent to therapists and religious leaders for conversion interventions were associated with depression, suicidal thoughts, suicidal attempts, less educational attainment, and less weekly income. Associations between SOCE, health, and adjustment were much stronger and more frequent for those reporting both attempts by parents and being sent to therapists and religious leaders, underscoring the need for parental education and guidance.”

Can some gay men and lesbians change their sexual orientation? 200 participants reporting a change from homosexual to heterosexual orientation

  • “This study tested the hypothesis that some individuals whose sexual orientation is predominantly homosexual can, with some form of reparative therapy, become predominantly heterosexual. The participants were 200 self-selected individuals (143 males, 57 females) who reported at least some minimal change from homosexual to heterosexual orientation that lasted at least 5 years.”
  • “The majority of participants gave reports of change from a predominantly or exclusively homosexual orientation before therapy to a predominantly or exclusively heterosexual orientation in the past year. Reports of complete change were uncommon.”
  • “there is evidence that change in sexual orientation following some form of reparative therapy does occur in some gay men and lesbians.”

 

There was an interesting case study in the 1970s which tried to “cure” a homosexual male through septal stimulation, it seemed to have worked in the short-term, but it is not clear whether this had long-term effects in changing the person’s sexual orientation.

 

SEPTAL STIMULATION FOR THE INITIATION OF HETEROSEXUAL BEHAVIOR IN A HOMOSEXUAL MALE

Bizarre Brain-Implant Experiment Sought to “Cure” Homosexuality

 

  • Case study of a homosexual male who underwent steptal stimulation in a hospital
  • Found that the researchers were able to get the male to become heterosexual as a result
  • It’s not clear whether this was a temporary or permanent change, however
